Beacon Patches
----------------------------------------------------------

This page contains links and instructions for a workaround to a known Internet Explorer bug that affects Weathernews' Beacon software.

Problem:

The current version of Beacon (1.4) can sometimes slow down or hang depending on the number of simultaneous users on a server. This problem is seen when using Internet Explorer on Windows-based PCs and is a result of a known Internet Explorer bug (related to the number of simultaneous open connections).

Fix Internet Explorer Problem Clicking on this link will download an application which will run and change two of your IE browser settings. This should not affect any of your other applications.
  • Windows XP Service Pack 2
    • when you click the link, you will be presented with a message that says "Do you want to run or save this file?" Please click "Run"
    • You will then be presented with a message that says that the "Publisher Can't be Verified." It will ask you if you are sure you want to run this software. Please click "Run" again.
    • You will be presented with a third message that describes the change to be made to your computer and prompts you to press "OK" or "Cancel." Please press "OK."
    • Finally your update will be complete. Please close Internet Explorer and restart it before accessing BEACON.
  • Windows 2000 Service Pack 4
    • when you click the link, you may be presented with a message that says "Do you want to open or save this file?" Please click "Open"
    • You will be presented with a second message that describes the change to be made to your computer and prompts you to press "OK" or "Cancel." Please press "OK."
    • Finally your update will be complete. Please close Internet Explorer and restart it before accessing BEACON.
